Welcome to my web-site !

My name is Ugo Lebreuilly and I am a post-doctoral researcher currently working at IRFU/DAP at CEA in Saclay.

I am currently working with Dr. Patrick Hennebelle as a post-doctoral staff of the ECOGAL project (founded by an ERC synergy).

My main current research topics are star/disk and planet formation and the overall dynamics of gas and dust in the interstellar medium. Before that, I did my Ph.D at the Centre de Recherche Astrophysique de Lyon on the ENS Lyon site under the direction of Benoît Commerçon. My thesis manuscript will be soon made available to the public.
